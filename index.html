Vinayam Nirmanam Infra

Construction with Integrity

Vinayam Nirmanam Infra (VNI) is a second-generation construction firm with roots in the industry since 1999. Built on decades of practical experience and a commitment to integrity, the firm has now been formally structured to expand its operations across multiple infrastructure sectors.

Over the years, the organisation has developed strong expertise in executing government contracts, particularly in the construction of roads, bridges, public buildings, and electrical infrastructure works. With a reputation for reliability, quality workmanship, and disciplined project execution, VNI continues to contribute to infrastructure development across the region.

While government infrastructure projects remain a core focus, the firm is now expanding its presence into private civil construction — including residential, commercial, and structural construction projects where the same standards of transparency, engineering precision, and professional management are applied.

With a legacy built on experience and a forward-looking approach, Vinayam Nirmanam Infra continues to grow as a trusted name in construction.
